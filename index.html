Some Info

The Red Hot Chili Peppers are an American rock band formed in Los Angeles in 1983. Their music incorporates elements of alternative rock, funk, punk rock and psychedelic rock. The band consists of vocalist Anthony Kiedis, bassist Flea,drummer Chad Smith, and guitarist John Frusciante. With over 100 million records sold worldwide, the Red Hot ChiliPeppers are one of the best-selling bands of all time
